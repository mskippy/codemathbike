2) Class Scavenger Hunt (20–25 min)

Download the Word document below and complete it with your partner/group. Make your answers stand out.

⬇️ Download: 1_1_Scavenger_Hunt.docx

How to save the file (OneDrive, correct folder)
  1. Open the download in Word.
  2. Go to File → Save As → choose your OneDrive.
  3. Create a folder named ICT_9 (if not already created).
  4. Save the file as 1_1_Scavenger_Hunt_YourName.docx in ICT_9.
What you’ll do (high-level)
  • Meet the people in your row and build a 1×4 table with names + one interesting thing for each person.
  • Locate key classroom items (e.g., garbage bin) and describe where to find them.
  • Open Microsoft Edge → navigate to your OneDrive.
  • Create a new Word file named 1_1_Twenty_Questions_About_Me.docx in your ICT_9 folder.
  • Copy the “Twenty Questions” (below) into that file and answer them.
  • Insert a picture of an origami butterfly (resize to ~2 in × 3 in).
  • Export your finished scavenger hunt to PDF and submit both the Word file and the PDF to MS Teams.
How to export as PDF (Word)
  1. In Word, go to File → Save As (or Export).
  2. Choose file type PDF (.pdf).
  3. Save to the same folder: ICT_9/1_1_Scavenger_Hunt_YourName.pdf.
How to submit to MS Teams
  1. Open the 1.1 Twenty Questions About Me assignment in MS Teams.
  2. Attach both files:
    • 1_1_Twenty_Questions_About_Me.docx
    • 1_1_Scavenger_Hunt_YourName.pdf
  3. Click Turn in.

Twenty Questions (copy into your Word file)

  1. Full name
  2. Preferred name
  3. Pronouns
  4. Favourite thing you did this summer
  5. Siblings?
  6. Pets?
  7. Semester 1 Courses – which subjects do you like the most?
  8. What are your hobbies/sports and how did you get into them?
  9. Create a 5-song playlist of your favourite songs at the moment.
  10. Tell me something interesting about yourself
  11. What goal are you working toward this semester?
  12. If you could visit any place in the world, where would it be and why?
  13. What is your dream job?
  14. What is your idea of a perfect vacation?
  15. What TV series would you binge watch over and over again and why?
  16. What is something you love learning about?
  17. Are you a programmer, gamer, or media designer? Do you even know?
  18. Mac or PC?
  19. What kind of devices do you have (tablet, laptop, desktop, console, phone, watch), and what do you use them for?
  20. What is something that you would like to learn in this course?
