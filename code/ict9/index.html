ICT 9

Course Outline

Welcome to ICT 9. This course will continue developing your digital literacy skills with a focus on creative and applied projects.

  • Unit 1: Game Development
  • Unit 2: Graphic Design

Class Expectations

  • Be prepared and on time
  • Save work regularly and keep organized folders
  • Respect equipment and classmates
  • Participate actively in lessons and projects

What We Will Do

Throughout the semester, we will explore:

  • Game Development: Learn to design and build interactive games.
  • Graphic Design: Use digital tools to create posters, logos, and visual media.
