ICT 9

Course Overview

Welcome to ICT 9! We’ll build creative and practical skills through a full game production cycle—designing a platform game, creating promotional assets, cutting a trailer, and publishing a simple website for your game. Along the way we’ll weave in brief mini-lessons on AI use and digital literacy.

  • U1 Intro & Digital Citizenship: course structures, expectations, 20-Questions survey, Canva “About Me”, Acceptable Use Agreement, intro to AI & digital-literacy norms.
  • U2 Game Development (GDevelop): set-up, movement, collisions, collectibles, enemies, levels, polish, export.
  • U3 Game Promotion (Poster): brand kit basics, poster layout (Adobe/Canva), copywriting, export for print/web.
  • U4 Web Development (Trailer + Site): plan and edit a short trailer, then ship a one-page game site and publish.

What We’ll Do

  • Build a side-scroll platformer in GDevelop.
  • Create a promotional poster and simple brand assets.
  • Edit a 30–60s game trailer with titles and music.
  • Publish a one-page website for your game and link your trailer.
  • Mini-lessons sprinkled throughout: smart/ethical AI use, file management, image/audio sourcing, and attribution.

Tip: Save frequently and keep your folders organized under H: or OneDrive.

Units
