Foundations for success in ICT 8

Welcome to Unit 1

In this unit, you’ll build essential digital skills: setting up your workspace, organizing files and folders, and practicing responsible habits (saving properly, naming clearly, and keeping things tidy). By the end, you’ll feel confident navigating your computer and managing your work efficiently.

Learning Goals (Student-Friendly)

  • I can find, open, and organize my files and folders.
  • I can save work to the correct place and name files clearly.
  • I can download resources, unzip them, and upload my work.
  • I can keep my workspace organized so I don’t lose marks (or time!).

Unit Objectives

  • Navigate the file system on your computer.
  • Create, organize, rename, and move files and folders.
  • Download, unzip, and upload files correctly.
  • Adopt effective digital habits for saving and backing up work.
